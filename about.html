About

Hello World, my name is Felipe, I am 40 years old. I was born and raised in Buenos Aires Argentina. I finished my studies as an industrial design technician, at ORT Schools. In 1998, I did the last year of high school in Chicago USA, where I felt in love with architecture and great masters who have their works in the city. Upon my return to Buenos Aires, I entered the Faculty of Architecture, Design and Urbanism (FADU). During my studies I had the opportunity to travel around the world and visit the great works of the great Master of Design. Currently living in beautiful city of Tel Aviv, Israel. Studying at Israel Tech Challenge (ITC), to become a FrontEnd Developer. Lover of the Bauhaus school design idea. Influenced by the great Master of Design such as Le Corbusier, Frank Lloyd Wright, Mies Van der Rohe, Tadao Ando, Zaha Hadid, Phillips Starck and Santiago Calatrava among others. I consider myself a Google search professional who is constantly on the lookout for simplicity and clarity. And from the idea that less is more <=>...
